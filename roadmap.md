# dharma.gift roadmap

*working draft, 2026-07-17. a side quest kevin is holding lightly while the team runs the gitcoin reboot. status: v0 live, first feedback loop running.*

## what it is

a web tool that takes daniel's ideas on dharma, sense-making, and the metacrisis and makes them beautiful, accessible, and shareable. visuals, subtle moloch nudges in the design, a telegram group hanging off it, an early launch video cut as of jul 16.

the bet: access to these ideas shouldn't be limited to people privileged enough to have personal access to daniel. the counter-pressure: daniel is deliberate about what gets publicly associated with him, so the tool may need to sit at arms length from him and CRI.

## where it sits strategically

rebuild.how frames ai livelihood as income + agency + purpose. dharma.gift is a purpose-leg experiment. it's also a live test of extitutional pedagogy (cohorts of people wandering through the questions together) vs institutional pedagogy (a teacher transmitting to students). same thesis as ethereum extitutional, different domain.

## the funnel (reordered per this week's feedback)

the v0 threw people into the metacrisis. the roadmap inverts it:

1. **sensing.** how do you actually feel, raw and embodied?
2. **sense-making.** how do you make sense of what you're sensing?
3. **dharma.** self-knowing and wholeness as prerequisite to doing work that protects and honors the sacred
4. **metacrisis.** the advanced layer, gated behind the earlier ones. internal parts-conflict scaled up into civilizational dynamics

keeping the front of the funnel at sensing/sense-making/dharma keeps it accessible to a much larger crowd. metacrisis is graduate school.

## phase 0: validation and positioning (now through end of july)

- reorder the site to the sensing → sense-making → dharma → metacrisis progression
- stay longer with the dharma question. link daniel's blog post, surface the actual inquiry questions, link consilience material for the sense-making layer
- get pointed ontological review from someone in the CRI training lineage. some elements are currently oversimplified; where they must stay simple, link out to the deeper material (articles, videos, podcasts) so simplicity stays honest
- resolve the arms-length question with daniel/CRI: what's blessed, what's independent, what's branded how. this gates everything downstream
- sit with the container question: kevin isn't a dharma teacher and shouldn't larp one. the honest posture is fellow wanderer. the cohort model may resolve this (the group holds the container together), and it's an open pondering rather than a settled answer

## phase 1: top of funnel (august)

- short form video > long text for lead gen. polish the launch video, cut it into short clips
- beachhead audience: viewers of nate's podcast. they already understand the metacrisis and are looking for guidance. hold off on the general public
- one clear call to action: enter the inquiry, join the group

## phase 2: community and cohorts (august through september)

- build the sharing loop: let people share their dharma inquiry responses with each other, or surface small great quotes from inquiries (anon or credited) on the site
- evolve the telegram group into a cohort model: small groups wandering through the questions together on a cadence. extitutional pedagogy as the actual mechanism
- support-in-processing: the inquiries surface real material for people. the community needs to be able to hold that, which is another reason cohorts beat a raw feed

## phase 3: depth and integration (q4)

- open the metacrisis layer properly for people who've moved through the earlier layers: parts-conflict and dissociation as the root of human-made problems, technology and alignment as the questions downstream of that
- deepen the resource graph: every simplified element links to its full-depth source
- explore integration with the rest of the stack: rebuild.how (purpose leg), regenhub programming (irl cohorts in boulder), possibly intentional-tech tools in the same design space
- revisit the arms-length question with real usage data in hand

## metrics (light, this is not a growth product)

- video views → site visits → completed inquiries → community joins
- cohort formation and retention beats raw signups
- qualitative bar: zero culty or manic vibes, zero oversimplification pushback from people deep in the lineage, at least a few unprompted "this changed how i'm sitting with my work" notes

## risks

- **the mcmindfulness trap.** consumerizing dharma into a productivity tool divorced from its depth. mitigation: link depth everywhere, cohorts over content, ontological review before scale
- **blowback onto daniel.** he's deliberate about what gets publicly associated with him. mitigation: arms-length structure, resolve phase 0 before any real distribution
- **oversimplification.** dangerous in this domain specifically. mitigation: keep it short but always linked to the deeper material
- **kevin's attention.** this is a side quest while gitcoin reboots. mitigation: phases are small, community does the work in phase 2+, and the team has the reboot mostly covered

## open questions

1. does daniel want this to exist at arms length or under CRI's umbrella? (gates everything)
2. what's the right community surface: telegram only, or inquiry-sharing built into the site?
3. is dharma the right word for the audience beyond nate's viewers, or does it stay zen-coded in a way that limits reach?
4. who else should pressure-test this before wider distribution?
