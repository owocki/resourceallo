# dharma.gift 🧘

**a visual map of humanity's deepest patterns — [live at dharma.gift](https://dharma.gift)**

designed to help people understand civilization, find their deepest role (dharma), and work together to steer the future upward. a wisdom school in alpha, wrapped around a living poster.

## the journey

- **[/](https://dharma.gift)** — the front door: a monk breathing under five trees while the city glows behind him. the sky matches your local time. his breath feeds the roots below; the door at the bottom opens the depth.
- **[/orient](https://dharma.gift/orient)** — where you get your bearings: what this is, how to walk it (*notice · savor · return*), and every door on one page — the fifteen questions, the zoom-out views, the crises, the skills.
- **[/spiral](https://dharma.gift/spiral)** — the main event: 12,000 years of who-gets-what as one living coil. 1,063 mechanisms, every one explained. fifteen question posters + thirteen zoom-out views ride the same page as tabs — including **[/metacrisis](https://dharma.gift/metacrisis)**, a scroll-driven tour of why the crises are one pattern.
- **[/crises](https://dharma.gift/crises)** (the census) · **[/skills](https://dharma.gift/skills)** (the inner game) · **[/about](https://dharma.gift/about)** (why this exists) · **[/grove](https://dharma.gift/grove)** and **[/crows](https://dharma.gift/crows)** (earlier front doors, still standing)

## how it's built

no build step, no bundler, no external js. the whole site is static files in **`html/`**, served as the site root by vercel (`"outputDirectory": "html"` + `cleanUrls`). each page is self-contained; the only network fetch is a google font.

run it locally:

```sh
python3 serve.py          # → http://localhost:8000
```

social cards are generated by `cardgen.py` (writes `cards/*.html`; screenshot each at 1200×630 into `html/og-<name>.png`). deeper conventions live in `CLAUDE.md`.

## contributing

PRs welcome — it's alpha, and the paint is wet on purpose.

1. fork, then `git clone` your fork
2. `python3 serve.py` and make your change in `html/` (small, focused PRs land fastest)
3. check your page in **dark and light**, at phone width, and with reduced motion on
4. if you add a page: give it its own social card (see `CLAUDE.md`, "the one hard rule") and a door on `/orient`
5. open a PR against `master` with a one-line description of what changed and why

not sure where to start? come say hi in the [telegram](https://t.me/+JeffHeYyATMzMDAx).
