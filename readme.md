# resource allocation through history ✨

**a living poster — [live at upwardspiral.io](https://www.upwardspiral.io)**

> 🖼️ **like it? [buy the physical poster](https://store.gitcoin.co/products/resource-allocation-history-framed-photo-paper-poster)** — the original "resource allocation through history" print, framed, from the gitcoin store.

An interactive remake of the Gitcoin "resource allocation through history" poster — 12,000 years of deciding *who gets what, and who decides*. Fourteen sibling posters are folded in as tabs: **[who decides](https://www.upwardspiral.io/power) · [how you eat](https://www.upwardspiral.io/live) · [what is true](https://www.upwardspiral.io/truth) · [who you are](https://www.upwardspiral.io/identity) · [who you trust](https://www.upwardspiral.io/trust) · [who keeps the record](https://www.upwardspiral.io/record) · [how you fight](https://www.upwardspiral.io/conflict) · [what can be proven](https://www.upwardspiral.io/math) · [what you owe](https://www.upwardspiral.io/ethics) · [what powers you](https://www.upwardspiral.io/energy) · [who heals you](https://www.upwardspiral.io/care) · [who owns the place](https://www.upwardspiral.io/land) · [who sets the clock](https://www.upwardspiral.io/time) · [what exists](https://www.upwardspiral.io/ontology)** — potlatch to quadratic funding, chiefdoms to forkable states, songlines to zero-knowledge proofs, wergild to peace bonds, tally marks to validity proofs, firewood to local energy markets, the midwife to a wage for the caregiver, the commons to the land value tax, the monastery bell to a clock nobody owns, animism to latent space. Each one explained.

Click around. It sparkles.

## features

- `index.html` — fifteen tabs, 1058 mechanisms: **allocation** (81), **power structures** (80), **livelihood** (77), **energy** (76), **care** (75), **time** (73), **mathematics** (72), **land** (71), **ethics** (70), **ontology** (70), **truth** (68), **identity** (66), **trust** (60), **conflict** (60), **the record** (59)
- **the whole picture** is five views now: 🧬 **the spiral** — all of it at once, with a camera that argues the shape (tilt it flat: from above it is a circle, from the side it is a climb, and only the low angle holds both) · ⚙️ **[one turn](https://www.upwardspiral.io/turn)** — the loop dissected face-on: the exit-cost ribbon, thick where you are trapped, and the capital beads jamming exactly where leaving is dear · 🪜 **[the ratchet](https://www.upwardspiral.io/ratchet)** — the four fires between the five eras, what each one burned (thrones) and what clicked through (tools) · 🌀 **[syntropy](https://www.upwardspiral.io/syntropy)** — what resists entropy, at eight scales · 🧩 **[assembly](https://www.upwardspiral.io/assembly)** — the ontological building blocks under every spiral: assembly theory ([cronin & walker](https://assemblytheory.xyz)) says the complexity of a thing is the fewest steps to build it from parts you already have. a scroll-driven walkthrough (a pinned hero, same lineage as the spiral and syntropy) takes the corporation apart into its bricks and then **fuses** the sprawling 14-step naïve tree down to its true assembly index of 7 — reuse, made visible — then hands you a ladder to build any target yourself; read the whole spiral as one assembly index going up, era by era
- Every mechanism explained on click — what it is, where it came from, its tradeoffs
- Each era ends with the **unbuilt ones** ✦ — specs waiting for a builder
- **[what powers you](https://www.upwardspiral.io/energy)** is the other rate limiter: no city without the granary, no cathedral without the ox, no model without the gigawatt. It ends where Jevons left it — we have never, not once, used less
- **[who heals you](https://www.upwardspiral.io/care)**, **[who owns the place](https://www.upwardspiral.io/land)** and **[who sets the clock](https://www.upwardspiral.io/time)**: the healer migrates from kin to insurer and the largest care system on earth is still on nobody's budget; the land claim migrates from presence to token and every transfer, followed back, is a transfer from somebody who was there; the clock migrates from sun to block, and the future still gets exactly one vote — the discount rate
- **[what exists](https://www.upwardspiral.io/ontology)** is the question under all the nouns: the carver migrates from myth to scripture to science to schema to model, and the misfit is where the power shows — the platypus was declared a hoax, the "other" box is still a dead end, and the newest ontology (latent space) has no boxes at all, and no way to say why
- **[what can be proven](https://www.upwardspiral.io/math)** is the rate limiter under all the others: no insurance before probability, no corporation before limited liability, no quadratic funding before somebody gave the square root a job. The advanced capital structures live here too — the commenda and the waqf, then limited liability and the share, then the derivative and the index fund, then the bonding curve and the hypercert
- **[what you owe](https://www.upwardspiral.io/ethics)** is the question that always arrives late. Its five hardest entries — the ones that were, in their own era, the ordinary and respectable view — sit behind a switch, off by default
- Every tab has its own crawlable URL + social card, so links unfurl properly
- Light & dark themes, responsive layout, reduced-motion friendly
- Self-contained HTML files — no build step, no dependencies

## running locally

It's just files. Open `index.html` in a browser, or serve the folder with anything:

```sh
python3 -m http.server
```
